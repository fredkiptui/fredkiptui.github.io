About Techmsafi

Techmsafi (Tech + Msafi) is a Nairobi-based creative studio focused on clean design and smart code. We help small businesses and consultancies build strong brand presence and fast websites.

Vision
To become Africa’s leading creative tech partner—empowering businesses with clean, smart digital solutions.
Mission
We merge creativity with technology to design future-ready brands and websites that deliver results.
